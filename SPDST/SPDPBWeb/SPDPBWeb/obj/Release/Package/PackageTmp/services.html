Application Overview

KROOSHI is a cloud-based mobile web application designed to revolutionize agricultural processes and foster seamless interactions among farmers, merchants, traders, companies, dealers, and distributors.

Our platform offers a comprehensive end-to-end solution, empowering farmers with a variety of essential functionalities.

Our platform is designed to meet the needs of all stakeholders in the agricultural sector. With a user-friendly interface accessible via mobile devices and web browsers, KROOSHI ensures convenience and accessibility for users regardless of their location or device.

Target Audience - Farmers

Services

  • Purchasing Inputs
    Buy seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and other agricultural inputs from merchants, traders, companies, dealers, and distributors.
  • Selling Produce
    Sell farm produce directly to buyers.
  • Hiring Labor
    Access labor hiring services to meet farm labor needs.
  • Access to Information
    Stay updated with weather reports, market prices, and advertisements related to agricultural tools and technological innovations.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Buying and Selling
    A platform to purchase inputs and sell produce seamlessly.
  • Hiring Labor
    Streamline the process of hiring labor for various agricultural tasks.
  • Advertisements
    Browse ads related to agricultural tools and technological advancements.
  • Product Listings
    Access a comprehensive listing of products from merchants, companies, dealers, and vendors.
  • Information Access
    Get up-to-date weather reports, market prices, and product listings to make informed decisions.

Target Audience - Merchants, Traders, Companies, Dealers, and Distributors

Services

  • Marketplace Transactions
    Buy and sell agricultural products seamlessly.
  • Product Listings
    List offerings and promote products and services.
  • Communication Channels
    Facilitate inquiries, negotiations, and collaborations with farmers.
  • Advertisements
    Showcase agricultural tools, equipment, and technological innovations to reach a wider audience.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Marketplace
    Conduct transactions and exchange agricultural products efficiently.
  • Product Listings
    Reach a broader audience by listing products and services.
  • Communication
    Establish effective communication with farmers for inquiries and collaborations.
  • Promotion
    Advertise agricultural tools and technological innovations to enhance visibility and sales.

By addressing these needs, KROOSHI enhances efficiency, transparency, and connectivity within the agricultural ecosystem, ultimately contributing to the growth and sustainability of the industry.
